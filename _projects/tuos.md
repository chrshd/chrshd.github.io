  - heading: What I did
    text: |
      - Led creative direction, design, and end-to-end project management across multiple annual prospectuses, working closely with the university throughout the project lifecycle
      - Worked within the university’s existing design system while developing extended style guides covering typography, colour, and imagery
      - Attended user research through focus groups, prioritising accessibility and inclusive design outcomes
      - Managed the full print production process, collaborating directly with printers to ensure quality and consistency
      - Developed and presented successful pitches to senior stakeholders, securing and maintaining the contract for 11 years
