  - text: |
      Established in 2012, CPU Records is an electronic music label recognised for its distinctive visual identity. Aligning with the label’s concept, I developed a scalable binary-based design system in which catalogue numbers function as the brand across all releases.

  - image:
    - /assets/cpu-0.jpg

  - heading: What I did
    text: |
      - Applied the design system across many physical formats, including vinyl, cassettes, MiniDiscs and floppy disks
      - Adapted the system to print and digital outputs: posters, books, audio equipment branding, video and live performance visuals
      - Created illustrations for merchandise and special editions, further extending the visual identity
      - Contributed to the label’s recognition in major platforms including Resident Advisor, Bleep and DJMag
      - Supported the label’s growth to 100+ releases and an international roster of artists
