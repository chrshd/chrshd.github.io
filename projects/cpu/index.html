Central Processing Unit

Project & delivery management Creative direction Identity Graphic design

Established in 2012, CPU Records is an electronic music label recognised for its distinctive visual identity. Aligning with the label’s concept, I developed a scalable binary-based design system in which catalogue numbers function as the brand across all releases and platforms.

What I did

  • Applied the design system across many physical formats, including vinyl, cassettes, MiniDiscs and floppy disks
  • Extended the system to print and digital outputs: posters, books, audio equipment branding, video and live performance visuals
  • Created illustrations for merchandise and special editions, extending the visual identity
  • Contributed to the label’s recognition in major platforms including Resident Advisor, Bleep and DJMag
  • Supported the label’s growth to 100+ releases and an international roster of artists
