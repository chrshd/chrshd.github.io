Festival of the Mind

Project & delivery management Identity Graphic design

Launched in 2012 by the University of Sheffield, Festival of the Mind brings together researchers and the city’s cultural and creative industries to present academic research through exhibitions, films, performances, workshops and talks. I worked on the festival’s visual identity across six biennial editions between 2012 and 2024.

What I did

  • Developed a flexible identity system designed to adapt to multiple formats while maintaining a coherent and accessible visual structure
  • Worked with 3D designers to align imagery and typography, ensuring consistency and clarity across all outputs
  • Collaborated with the University’s Cultural Engagement team to deliver a city-wide advertising campaign
  • Managed rollout across large-format outdoor media and digital channels, applying the identity across billboards, buses, exhibition signage, programmes, apparel and digital marketing outputs, with a focus on accessibility, legibility and navigation to maximise public reach and engagement
  • Iteratively refined the identity across successive editions to improve clarity and programme communication
