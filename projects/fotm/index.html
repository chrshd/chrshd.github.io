Festival of the Mind

Identity Graphic design Delivery Project management

Launched in 2012 by the University of Sheffield, Festival of the Mind brings together researchers and the city’s cultural and creative industries to present academic research through exhibitions, films, performances, workshops and talks. I worked on the festival’s visual identity across six biennial editions between 2012 and 2024.

I worked with 3D designers to align imagery and typography, maintaining consistency across a range of outputs. The identity functioned as a flexible system, adapting to different formats while retaining a coherent visual structure.

In collaboration with the University’s Cultural Engagement team, I managed and delivered a full-scale advertising campaign across large-format outdoor media and digital channels, alongside exhibition signage, programmes and apparel.

The identity was applied across the city through print and digital outputs and refined across successive editions to improve clarity and communication of the programme.
