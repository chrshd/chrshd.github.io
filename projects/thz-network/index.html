EPSRC NetworkPlus in Terahertz Systems

UI/UX Interaction design Full stack development
Visit site

I led end-to-end design and development for the Network Plus in Terahertz Systems Science, a website enabling academics and research professionals to sign up, create profiles and connect. The project required a clear, accessible structure to support onboarding, discovery and ongoing engagement.

Working with stakeholders, I defined the site map, core layout and interface patterns, making considered decisions on content design to ensure clarity and consistency across the platform. I created interactive prototypes to define user journeys and refine interactions, ensuring the experience was intuitive and accessible from the outset.

I then developed both the front and back end of the site, translating the designs into a robust and maintainable build. The development focused on performance, scalability and ensuring the system could support user profiles, onboarding and search functionality effectively.

Accessibility was a key consideration throughout. I applied established patterns, referencing GOV.UK design standards for forms, search and filtering, to ensure the site was intuitive, inclusive and easy to navigate.
